Who am I?

My name is Alexander Fagrell and I'm a software developer enthusiast and do not give up until I know the ins and outs of how something works. In addition to software development, I enjoy coaching people, both on a professional as well as a personal level, in order to improve productivity and to create a positive working environment. I'm from Stockholm, Sweden, but currently live and work in London, UK, for a security company as a lead software engineer.

These are some of my skills and expertise:
  • Experience in recruiting, hiring, mentoring and managing a team of software engineers to improve their skills and productivity.
  • Experience in scoping, planning and prioritisation of key projects as well as suggests and implements architectural design.
  • Experience in monitoring a team's performance as well as conducting code reviews.
  • Extensive experience in modern C++(C++11/14/17) software development including STL, Qt (Widget and Quick) and Boost libraries.
  • Experience developing multi-threaded cross-platform applications for Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, Android and QNX 6.X.
  • Experience in C, C#, Python and Objective C development
  • Experience in CUDA, GPUDirect and OpenGL, especially for video processing.
  • MSc degree in Mechatronics
